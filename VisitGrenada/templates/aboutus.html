About Us

Visit Grenada is a small project made by me for college. I chose the idea of Visit Grenada due to my family being from Grenada. And having spent several summers there, I know first hand how beautiful and serene Grenada is.

One of my fondest memories is walking up and down the beach you see on the right, Grand Anse, watching beautiful sunsets and feeling the sand on my feet, and hearing the ocean waves crash against the shore, there truly is no better place on Earth.

I've also explored Grenada, land and sea, and even the Jungle. I've explored shipwrecks and got my diving certificates, relaxed at the most serene beaches possible, and spent time deep in the island's mountains with monkeys on my shoulder.
